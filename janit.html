About us

The dream and commitment to develop Takshila Public School started in 2000 to provide quality education at school level.
The school is a co-educational institution on Public School lines and imparts education in English Medium. It admits puplis as day scholars, as well as boarders. Education can, and should both a religious and an enjoyable process for the child. Children are encouraged to learn for themselves under the teachers guidance.
TPS impart education which is prefect blend of Indian culture, tradition and the knowledge required to suit the modern era. "We do not prepare your ward for the exams, we prepare them for life.
