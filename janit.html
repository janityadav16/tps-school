About us

The dream and commitment to develop Takshila Public School started in 2000 to provide quality education at school level. The school is a co-educational institution on Public School lines and imparts education in English Medium. It admits puplis as day scholars, as well as boarders. Education can, and should both a religious and an enjoyable process for the child. Children are encouraged to learn for themselves under the teachers guidance. TPS impart education which is prefect blend of Indian culture, tradition and the knowledge required to suit the modern era. "We do not prepare your children for the exams, we prepare them for life".

Achivement

In the realm of academia, achievement is a beacon that illuminates the path of every student's journey. It thrives in the bonds forged with peers and teachers, where individuals learn the value of teamwork and empathy, nurturing an environment where success is shared and celebrated together. The school experience embraces achievement in its multifaceted glory, shaping well-rounded individuals poised to make their mark on the world.
Amidst the tapestry of achievement, we find the heart of our school community beating passionately. Quiet achievers humbly traverse the path of self-improvement, their unwavering determination serving as an inspiration to others. Whether these small triumphs leave an indelible mark on the fabric of school life. As they approach each challenge with courage and enthusiasm, students learn that achievement is not solely about reaching summits but cherishing the growth, camaraderie, and life lessons discovered along the way. Our school's nurturing environment propels its learners towards greater heights, igniting a lifelong love for learning, and fostering the belief that with passion and perseverance, they can achieve greatness in whatever they set their hearts on.
